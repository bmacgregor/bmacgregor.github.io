Session Two : A Cryptic Tomb

As the drunk speaks, you discount a few things. First, your people do not participate in blood ritual - it is forbidden by the ancestors; spoken of once to younglings and then never again. To mention more than this is to call the dead gods back to the world. It is not done.

You are about to reproach the besotted fool when he dips his hand into a mug and scrawls out a number of glyphs on the bar in front of you. He draws three, and their names are Ancient, Undying, and Breath.

You feel your brows knit together. How does such a person know these things? Why would the tribes venture this far south? For these are indeed Kevanjic runes, and old ones at that. Perhaps this tomb bears investigation. You turn your attention back to the drunk.
