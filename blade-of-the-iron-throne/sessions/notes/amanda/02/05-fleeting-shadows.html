Session Two : Fleeting Shadows

These are the words of your people, but they are profane - the stuff of madness, that would unshackle the dead from their bonds, and give birth to uncountable horrors, and these words are cut into the living stone - a wound in the world itself.

You turn to regard the dead thing behind you - once mortal, but now a disgusting parody of your people's beliefs, steeped in the darkest of Kevanjic lore and theology.

Looking closer, you notice a small brand on the thing's exposed forearm - a hooked cross held by a gloved fist. Your preternatural senses pulse, and instinctively you step back from the corpse. With your third eye, you see a shadow peel itself from the dead thing, and turn to regard you. It shrieks, unleashing a terrible laughter, and is gone in an instant.
