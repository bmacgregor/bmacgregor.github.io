Session Two : House Garont

Stepping into the central chamber, your eyes flick to the a number of human corpses littering the ground. Normally, such a sight would hardly raise an eyebrow, but this is different.

You know these men.

To be precise, you don't know their names. You've never met them, but you know them all the same. Their colours, muddled by grisly wounds, mark them as men of House Garont - a minor Eldrani lordling.

These are your countrymen. But why are they here? There are questions, it seems, that you need answers to.
