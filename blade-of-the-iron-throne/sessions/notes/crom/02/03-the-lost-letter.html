Session Two : The Lost Letter

Just as Percel instructed, your dead kinsmen are here; four of them, bodies bloated and rotting in the dark, their shields emblazoned with the crest of house Garont - a minor Eldrani lordling. It's unlikely that any of your companions recognize the mark, a hawk on the wing, clutching a broken sword.

Your eyes flick from the corpses to the spiders, each as large as a grapefruit, all huddled around one of their dead, a monstrous beast nearly the size of a child. You scan the corpses again, and notice a sheet of parchment tucked into a belt - surely this is the dispatch you were sent for!

You will have to be quick if you intend on stealing the missive. Luckily, the spiderlings should distract your company long enough for you to pilfer it.
