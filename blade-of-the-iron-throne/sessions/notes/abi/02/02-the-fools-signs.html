Session Two : The Fool's Signs

You're not accustomed to being in such a place - the smells and light threaten to overwhelm your senses, given as they are to night and dark. For some reason, you let yourself be dragged away with the foreigner and the drunk, and your patience has almost run out. About to snarl a cutting farewell to the both of them, something stops you - with a fluid grace that belies his besotted state, the man dips a hand into a mug of ale and casually drags his fingers across the bar, drawing out three strange glyphs.

You know these markings. These were etched above the wardstone of your coven and at the entrance to every chamber. Their meaning was lost to time, but the memory of them causes you to gasp in shock. What could this drunk fool know of your kind?

Suddenly, you find yourself quite interested in the rest of the man's tale...
