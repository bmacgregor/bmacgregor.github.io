Session Two : A Quagmire of Wraiths

The last of the wraiths fall, your sword shuddering as it impacts a spinal column. The fool, it seems, has led you to the exact place you have been seeking; this appears to be a coven-house.

Well, not a coven-house exactly. These sorry wretches were trapped here by some design, but this is a place that reminds you of your home, burrowed underground and warm. It seems like such a waste for the livestock to have gone insane - normally, thralls such as the dozen dead that lie in heaps at your feet are docile - living to serve, and be fed upon. Perhaps, you reflect, their time imprisoned here snapped their tiny minds, and caused them to go feral.

One thing you are certain of, however, is the presence of another, like you. Somewhere deep inside this tomb lurks a creature of great power and age; though if its thralls had lost their minds, how much worse might their master be?
