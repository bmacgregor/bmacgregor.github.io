Session Two : The Beast's Lair

The sweet smell of decay is heavy in the air as you step into the central chamber. In the corner are several uman corpses dressed in unfamiliar colours. Your education had you learn the common highborn livery of most of the Fornish noble houses. These poor fools are not from Fornholm - or if they are, the lord they owe alliegance to is petty enough that you never learned their name.
