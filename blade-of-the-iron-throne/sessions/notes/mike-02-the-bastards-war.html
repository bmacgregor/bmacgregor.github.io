Session Two : The Bastard's War

After glancing at your stolen map, you realize that you must be close to the Eldrani border and to the Bastard's War by proxy. It's unlikely that Ranfulf knows of his father's death, or the mud that the Council of Lords has been slinging to blacken his name. If you're going to help your old commander win his crown, you're going to need to make sure he knows what's going on in his country.

Whitelake, a small fishing village, is nearby - if you and your group push hard, you might be able to make it there by daybreak. With any luck, they might have a courier there that could get word to Ranulf, and perhaps you might be able to stem the tide of rumors that threaten his credibility and his rightful inheritance.
