Session Two : Scores and Grudges

Peering down into the camp, you note a few tactical positions of advantage. The bandits are set up well - definitely not the run-of-the-mill rabble, but they have a few choke-points you and your group could exploit. Hod points to a figure in the distance coming out of a large pavillion.

You know him.

Pims - the renegade scum, partly responsible for your current situation and the deaths of your friends. You cast your gaze around, looking for Sirtley and Ornstone, or better, Dawint, the bastard in charge, who slew your companions, and who you owe a debt of pain.

Tessins shakes his head. "Just Pims then," you say. Tessins shrugs. Pims, it seems, will have to do for now.
