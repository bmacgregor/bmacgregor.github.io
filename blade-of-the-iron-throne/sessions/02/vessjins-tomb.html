Vessjin's Tomb

The room beyond is revolting - covered in filth and grime. Huddled in the corner are several figures, who rise at your entrance. One of them, steps toward you and into the torchlight. What you witness is a nightmare - worse even than the wights you encountered at the entrance. This creature stands nearly seven feet tall, though it would be even taller if it stood straight. Its pale and bloodless frame moves with a horrid grace, and the clothes it once wore are tattered. Its companions are no better - all of them female incarnations of the towering horror that makes its spidery way toward you.

"At lassssst," the thing whispers out, its voice dry and papery - "at long last, we will be fed! Come my darlings! Vessjin hungers, and these cattle will serve us well!" And the figure leaps toward you with terrific speed, whilst his brides surge from the stone floors and attack!

Neither Vessjin, nor his brides wear any kind of armour. Vessjin will attack the first person that entered the chamber, and the brides will each pair off with a single PC - there are as many brides as needed to do this.

Vessjin

A ravenous vampire, though weakened by his long imprisonment. Vessjin will attack with claws and fangs, and his long reach and incredible speed allow him to attack from medium range with the claws, and short range with his fangs.
