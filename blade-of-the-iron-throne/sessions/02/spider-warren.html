Session Two : Spider Warren

The Spider Warren

You and your group wander rougly eastward, using the starts as a guide, the frosted snows cracking underfoot as you wind your way toward Whitelake. You've been marching steadily for a few hours, and dawn is fast approaching. The forest has gradually been thinning, giving way to rolling plains of the far east of Fornholm and its uneasy border with Eldran, across the Weeper's Channel. You trudge past another hip-deep snowdrift and through the trees, and find yourself in an odd clearing, full of strange cave-like openings, almost large enough for a man to walk through. In all receed back into the ground and absolute blackness.
