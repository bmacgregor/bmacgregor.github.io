Session Two : Return to the Warren

Return to the Warren

The place is much as you left it, seven opening that receed into the darkness of the earth found in a small clearing. Two of the caves are fairly large, while the others would be tight indeed to squeeze through. You doubt that you would have much room to swing your weapons freely, though stabbing and thrusting would probably serve you in the cramped quarters.

If the Players arrive at the nest around nightfall, the monstrous snow spider will be out hunting, and unlikely to return for some time. The Players will have to contend with her nine younglings, however, which are active in the heart of the cavern. All of the "arms" of the cavern join up in the centre, where the Players will be able to find four dead Eldrani soldiers, identifiable by their shields and armour, though only Crom will know what house they come from and their purpose in the hive.

Spider, Youngling

Not an earthly spider, but one with a body at least the size of a grapefruit. It has a chitinous exoskeleton that protects it like hardened leather (1 Piercing & Cleaving). Its murderous poison has a progression rate of a few minutes, and requires a Quality of Success of 2 to succeed at the Progression Check.

Attributes

  • Daring 4
  • Tenacity 3
  • Cunning 5
  • Reflex 5
  • Knockdown 4
  • Knockout 3
  • Move 8 (+4)

Melee Pool

  • Small Fangs 6
