Session Two : The Sunken Temple

The Sunken Temple

You travel, following Farstin's direction. Though drunk, you're surpised at how accurate the landmarks and estimates of distance are; it appears that your inebriated friend has a knack for accuracy.

Several hours pass, as you and your companions march through the winter wilds, and eventually, you find the narrow pass that Farstin mentioned. Following it for a few hundred yards, it widens gradually. The snow underfoot crackles with every step, and you realize that the ground is littered with bones - at first, hardly enough to cause alarm, but as you progress, there seems to be more - hundreds, if not thousands or shards, bleached by the sun and weathered by the cold. The ravine finally ends - progress blocked by a grotto, sunken into the earth.
