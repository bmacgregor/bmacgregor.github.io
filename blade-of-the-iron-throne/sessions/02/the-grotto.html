Session Two : The Grotto

The Grotto

The air is thick down here, and the dark swallows the tiny light of your torch. Somewhere in the distance you hear a faint rattle, the scruff of bare feet on the hard earth, and then a scream as a dozen figures run into the flickering light, their eyes round with madness, and their skin pale and taught with the sure signs of starvation. The wights let loose another horrible shriek, and immediately attack.

Wight

The insane prisoners of Vessjin's Tomb, these wights were once the dread lord's servants and he would often feed from them. Their semi-immortality, however, has caused them to lose their minds in the centuries that they have been imprisoned with Vessjin.
