Session Two : Whitelake

Whitelake

You leave Palisin's hut, and head off to Whitelake. The familiar crunch of frost-covered snow underfoot crackles with every step, and you note the sun is lowering in the sky. It appears to be sometime in the mid-afternoon. With any luck, the people of Whitelake will be milling around town, or at the local inn - a place that almost every village of this size seems to have, and abound in rumors and idle gossip.

The journey is fairly short, and you and your group can wander the streets as you like. There doesn't seem to be a particularly strong law-enforcement here, and you suspect the town either handles its own issues through the use of impromptu courts and defence using a volunteer militia.

You can hear hints of conversation; someone mentions a handful of delerious travellers, suffering from some sort of wasting illness. Hopefully Whitelake isn't on the verge of a plague.

Now that you're all here, what would you like to do?

Players will likely split up, following their own threads. With any luck, Kerrin will go to investigate the rumors of plague - she will find seveal delerious Eldrani soldiers disguised as roving tradesmen looking for work at the local infirmary, being tended to by an elderly woman named Kass, detailed here.

Crom, from his note, will likely go in search of an Eldrani contact. He will find Alliver Percel, a local weaver and purveyor of fine linens who is a deepcover Eldrani agent placed on this side of the Channel, outlined here.

With little else to go with, Abi, Mike, and Amanda will likely head to the local inn and brewhouse, the Rose and Barrel where they will encounter a few colourful characters with plot hooks for them to investigate, described here.
