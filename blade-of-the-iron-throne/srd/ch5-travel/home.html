Chapter Five : Travel and Health

Chapter five covers travelling and scene framing (narrative guidelines for the Game Master). It also describes how Characters heal from wounds sustained in combat, and finally covers the optional Encumbrance rules.

  • Travel and Scene Framing is covered here.

  • Health and Healing is described here. This includes how Blood Loss is handled both in and out of combat, falling damage, generic damage (that is, damage sustained without an easily determined type, like fire for example), and poison. This section also includes the It's Just a Scratch optional rule.
  • The optional Encumbrance rules can be found here, and covers encumbrance for both Characters and Mounts.
