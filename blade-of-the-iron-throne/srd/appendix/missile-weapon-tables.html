Daggers cannot attain extreme range.

The dagger thrown to deadly effect is a mainstay of the Sword & Sorcery genre, and the stats reflect this. We strongly recommend that you use those stats for your game of Blade, but if you really can’t bring yourself to cater to this cliché, you can tone down the thrown dagger by changing its Damage Rating to –1.

Bows

All bows have a BN Attribute of their own, one related to their individual draw weight. Half of this BN is used to calculate damage instead of half the user’s BN. Nobody can use a bow possessing a higher BN rating than he himself possesses. Maximum BN possible for shortbows is 4, for bows 5, and for longbows and composite bows 6.
