Chapter Six : Sorcery

This chapter provides rules for Sorcery, including how multiple sorcerers can combine their efforts and affecting multiple targets. It describes the lesser and greater Sorcerous Mysteries, and the rare Arcane Secrets that can be used by powerful Sorcerers. Finally, rules for running a Duel of Wills - sorcerous combat - are outlined.

  • For an overview on Sorcery, click here. This includes the mechanics of Sorcery, and gives the formula for how Power is calculated, as well as Target Numbers for casting spells. It also describes Taint and Containment Checks, Combining Sorcerous Efforts with multiple magic-users pooling their energies to cast a single spell, and Affecting Multiple Targets with a single spell.
  • A descriptive list of the Sorcerous Mysteries can be found here, which form the basis for spells and spellcasting.

  • Arcane Secrets are detailed here, magic used by only the most powerful of spell-casters.
  • Rules for conducting sorcerous combat, the Duel of Wills, can be found here. This section goes over how an arcane duel is conducted, and describes the Maneuvers availble to sorcerous combatants.
