Proficiencies Described

Some fighting styles are quite similar to others, and each Proficiency lists those styles that are closely related. These “Defaults” are designated with a negative Proficiency modifier based on the Proficiency from which the default originates.

Defaults are listed in the following table. Thus, if your primary Proficiency is Sword & Shield and you need to find the default for Greatsword, read the “defaults from Sword & Shield” column on the Greatsword line of the proficiencies table, and apply this modifier to your Sword & Shield rating. In this case it is –3, meaning that you can use the Greatsword Proficiency at 3 less than your Sword & Shield Proficiency.
