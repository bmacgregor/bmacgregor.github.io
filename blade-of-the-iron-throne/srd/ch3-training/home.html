Chapter Three : Training

This chapter described the six talents, or area of expertise that each Character has, and how to leverage these talents to create a Character. It also includes a talent table, which breaks down Priority levels, and details what each level gives to a Character once it has been purchased. Finally, an example of Character Creation is provided, as well as notes for making a custom talent table.
