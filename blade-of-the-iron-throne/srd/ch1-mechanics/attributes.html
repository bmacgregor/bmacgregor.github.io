Attribute Descriptions

In Blade, each Character is defined by a set of Attributes that form the core of that individual. There are six basic attributes and five secondary attributes derived from the basic attributes which further develop a Characters' strengths and weaknesses.

Basic Attributes

Basic Attributes, or just Attributes, are the character’s inherent physical, spiritual, and social capabilities that govern how he is perceived by others, how strong, fast, healthy, knowledgeable, and smart he is, and how well developed his instincts and social intelligence are. The six Basic Attributes are:

Brawn (BN)

Brawn is a measure of muscle, vitality, and a Characters frame. It indicates how much damage you do unaided, and how easily you resist punishment, disease, and poison.

Daring (DG)

Daring is a measure of courage and ability to take decisive action. It affects how well you can strike and parry, as well as how skilled you are with actions like climbing, acrobatics and all kinds of athletics.

Tenacity (TY)

Tenacity is the ability to focus on a task, a measure of the strength of your persona. It affects how easily influenced you are, your inner discipline, and your sanity.

Heart (HT)

Heart is a measure of social aptitude. It affects both how charismatic you appear to others (people and animals) and how easily you can get them to do your bidding. It governs your ability for empathy, and the understanding of human and animal nature.

Sagacity (SY)

Sagacity is a measure of mental agility. It indicates how logical, intuitive, and smart you are. It affects situations that require reasoning, sharpness of the senses, as well as any judgment relating to them.

Cunning (CG)

Cunning is a measure of raw instinct, agility, reaction time, and balance. It affects how skilled you are with actions like lock picking, pick pocketing, hiding, and sneaking.

Combined Attributes

Combined Attributes are calculated by combining two or more Basic Attributes. All numbers equal to or greater than 0.50 are rounded up. The Combined Attributes are:

Reflex

Reflex determines how quickly a character physically reacts to external stimulus.

  • Reflex = (Cunning + Daring) / 2

Aim

Aim quantifies one’s natural ability to hit a target over distances.

  • Aim = (Cunning + Sagacity) / 2

Knockdown

Knockdown is a measure of how solid and balanced one remains after taking a blow.

  • Knockdown = (Brawn + Daring) / 2

Knockout

Knockout is a measure of how hard it is to knock a character unconscious.

  • Knockout = (Brawn + Tenacity) / 2

Move

Move is a measure of how much distance one can cover on foot in a hurry.

  • Move = (Brawn + Cunning + Daring) / 3
