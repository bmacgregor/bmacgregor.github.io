Chapter One : Mechanics

This chapter covers what Role Playing Games are and describes Blade of the Iron Throne and how it's played. It goes over the types of rolls (called Checks) Characters will make during a game, different types of Checks that might be called for during play, and problems that might result from failing at something.

This chapter goes on to outline Character Attributes and what each one does, and also explains the concept of Passions and how they define a Character and inform their actions.

  • An introduction to Role playing Games (RPG's), as well as to Blade itself can be found here.
  • Major Checks as well as the types of dice used are described here. The Major checks include Attribute, Skill, and Proficiency Checks, as well as Opposed Checks.

  • Timed Checks are detailed here. Timed Checks are a variant of any type of Check, and are used when tracking progression is important.

  • Complications resulting from catastrophic failures are outlined here.
  • Both major and combined Attributes are defined here.

  • Passion Attributes, a cornerstone of the game, are discussed here.
