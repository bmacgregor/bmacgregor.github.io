Chapter Seven : Sword & Sorcery Gaming

This chapter acts as a primer for the Sword & Sorcery genre, covering general themes and tropes encountered, as well as providing Game Masters with a list of tips that may come in handy when running their own games.

  • For an overview of the Sword & Sorcery genre, click here. This covers the feel of most Sword & Sorcery worlds, gives an overview to how Faith, the Divine, and Magic are often presented, and also goes over the racial make-up of most Sword & Sorcery worlds. Heroes and Villains are described, and an idea of how most Sword & Sorcery tales are written is presented.

  • Twenty tips for running your own game of Blade are listed here. Finally, this section wraps up with the Iron Rule, which is critical to running a game of Blade.
